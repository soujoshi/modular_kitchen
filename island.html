The ultimate in modular kitchen design, the island shaped kitchen provides great functionality and appeal. An island
shaped kitchen is usually a U shaped kitchen with an island added to the center which allows for movement all around
it. The counter of the island can be used as a work surface or to place a sink or a cooking range or even as a seating or
dining arrangment.

When space is not an issue, the island shaped kitchen can create a wonderful platform for conversations and family
interactions not to mention the compliments that you make get from many a guests.The island shaped kitchen is the
one style to go for if you want to create a room out of your kitchen. Not only does it gives the flexibility to create new
designs, it provides room for activities and especially kids who might want to join you when you are working in the
kitchen.
